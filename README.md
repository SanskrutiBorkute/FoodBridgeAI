# Abstract

Food wastage is a major global issue, while millions of people continue to suffer from food insecurity. Restaurants, hotels, catering services, events, and households often generate surplus food that remains unused and eventually gets discarded.

FoodBridgeAI is an AI-powered food redistribution platform designed to connect food donors with NGOs and charitable organizations. The system uses Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to predict future food demand, prioritize food distribution, and improve resource allocation.

The platform aims to reduce food wastage, improve food accessibility, and support efficient food donation management through intelligent automation.

# 1. Introduction

Food waste is one of the most pressing social and environmental challenges worldwide. Large quantities of edible food are discarded daily despite increasing food demand among underprivileged communities.

Traditional food donation systems often lack proper coordination between donors and NGOs, leading to delayed distribution and inefficient resource utilization.

FoodBridgeAI provides a centralized platform where food donors can register surplus food and NGOs can access available donations. AI-based forecasting and prioritization models help ensure that food reaches the right beneficiaries efficiently.

# 2. Literature Review

Several food donation platforms and food bank systems have been developed to facilitate food redistribution. These systems primarily focus on connecting donors and recipients.

Recent advancements in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning have enabled predictive analytics and intelligent decision-making in logistics and resource management.

Research suggests that AI-based demand forecasting and prioritization can significantly improve resource allocation and reduce wastage.

These findings inspired the development of FoodBridgeAI, which integrates food donation management with AI-powered prediction and prioritization capabilities.

# 3. Methodology

The system consists of three major components:

## Food Donors

Donors can:

* Register food donations
* Provide food quantity and details
* Specify expiry information
* Share available food resources

## NGOs

NGOs can:

* Register on the platform
* View available donations
* Receive food recommendations
* Manage food collection activities

## AI Engine

The AI module:

* Predicts future food demand
* Calculates priority scores
* Supports intelligent food allocation
* Assists decision-making for distribution

This creates an efficient and intelligent food redistribution ecosystem.

## Working Process

1. Food donor registers a food donation.
2. Donation details are stored in the database.
3. NGOs register and become available for food allocation.
4. AI analyzes donation and NGO information.
5. Priority scores are generated.
6. Suitable NGOs are recommended.
7. NGOs collect available food.
8. Forecasting model predicts future food demand.
9. Analytics and insights are displayed on the dashboard.

# 6. Limitations

Although the platform performs effectively, some limitations remain:

* Transportation logistics are not automated.
* Food quality verification is manual.
* No real-time GPS tracking is currently implemented.
* User authentication is not yet integrated.
* AI accuracy depends on available training data.
* Internet connectivity is required.
